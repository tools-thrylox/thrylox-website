Thrylox Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice explains how Thrylox (“we”, “us”, “our”) processes information in connection with this website and our business relationships. This document is intended primarily for partners, vendors, platforms and financial institutions.

1. Data controller

Thrylox is operated by an independent game development company registered in the European Union. Full legal entity details (name, registration number, registered address) are available upon request to admin@thrylox.com.

2. What information we collect via this website

We intentionally keep this website minimal. We may process:

  • Technical information provided by your browser (IP address, device type, basic logs) for security and reliability.
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us by email (name, role, company, contact details, message content).

3. How we use this information

  • To respond to partnership, publishing, vendor or compliance enquiries.
  • To protect our services from abuse and security threats.
  • To meet legal, regulatory and contractual requirements.

4. Legal bases

Where EU/EEA data protection law applies, we rely on our legitimate interests in operating a secure business, performing or entering into contracts, and complying with legal obligations.

5. Sharing and storage

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We may share information with trusted service providers (e.g. hosting, email, analytics, payments) under appropriate safeguards.
  • Data may be stored or processed in the EU and other jurisdictions with reasonable security measures in place.

6. Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Your rights

Where applicable, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights or raise questions, contact us at admin@thrylox.com.

8. Cookies & tracking

This site currently does not use invasive third-party tracking for consumer profiling. Limited technical logs or analytics may be used to ensure availability and security.

9. Updates

We may update this notice from time to time. The latest version will always be available at thrylox.com/privacy.html.
