step 01 // take the contract

You are a Resolver, moving from conflict to conflict aboard your own ship.

In BOG, you are not queueing into anonymous matches. You travel as a sanctioned contract specialist, take on disputes that matter, and grow your name by settling them through official AR duel protocol.

Your ship It is your moving base of operations: the place you return to after a mission, collect rewards, and choose where to go next.
Your career Small disputes are only the beginning. As your reach grows, so do the stakes, the places you can travel to, and the kind of work only you can solve.

step 02 // build your signature collection

Collect striking figures, learn what they do, and bring the exact 12-danger answer.

Your figurines are not disposable units. They become your tools, your counters, and your signature style. Every duel asks you to read the situation, choose the right combination, and commit to it under a fixed limit.

Collection with meaning Each new figure expands your strategic vocabulary and changes the kinds of answers you can assemble for the next conflict.
Honest PvP The best duel is not won by frantic tapping. It is won by reading the opponent better, committing the sharper lineup, and understanding the system more deeply.

step 03 // descend into the anomaly

Beyond PvP, the world opens into tense PvE runs where a good build can snowball into something wild.

BOG is not only about fair duels. It also points toward anomaly runs: chained fights, hard choices between encounters, and the thrill of building momentum until your whole setup starts humming like an engine.

PvE that grows with you You survive encounter after encounter, shape the run with each choice, and feel a careful build turn into a dangerous one.
This build The current TestFlight slice is still compact, but it already lets you feel the role, the duel logic, the hub return, and the larger product waiting behind it.
