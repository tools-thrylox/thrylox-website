resolver fantasy

You are not a generic commander. You are a Resolver.

In BOG, conflict is not abstract menu combat. Disputes are settled through sanctioned AR duel protocol, and the player steps into that system as a respected specialist trusted to take the contract, read the situation, and deliver the right answer.

Your ship is not just a loading screen. It is your moving base of operations: the place you return to after a mission, collect rewards, choose where to travel next, and feel your career becoming larger than any single match.

honest pvp

Fair PvP built on drafting, counters, and lineup mastery

BOG is a team battler with honest PvP. The core pleasure is not frantic tapping. It is reading the opponent better, drafting with intent, and committing the sharper answer under constraint.

The 12-danger rule gives the whole duel its shape. You do not just throw strength onto the board. You assemble the exact amount of risk you can afford, balance counters and synergies, and accept the clarity of your own commitment.

collection

Collect figures that expand your strategic vocabulary

Your figurines are not disposable units. Each one changes the kinds of answers you can bring, the matchups you can solve, and the kind of player identity you build over time.

The fantasy is not only collecting something beautiful. It is collecting options, counters, and styles of judgment that make your roster feel more precise every time you return to the board.

roguelike pve

Beyond PvP, the game opens into escalating anomaly runs

BOG is not only about fair duels. It also points toward PvE runs where each encounter, reward, and risk choice pushes the build in a new direction.

The goal is that special engine-building feeling: a careful start turning into a dangerous machine, where the run begins to hum and every decision starts paying off in amplified ways.

career arc

Short sessions that feed a larger career

One contract should always point beyond itself. Resolve a dispute, claim the reward, return stronger, and see the next step already waiting: more travel, better figures, higher ranks, stranger anomalies, and a larger place in the world.
