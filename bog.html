project description // what bog is

You are a Resolver in a future system that settles conflict through sanctioned AR duels.

BOG is a mobile sci-fi strategy game centered on short-session, high-clarity AR duels. The player is not a generic commander. You are a respected contract champion trusted to resolve disputes through official duel protocol, read the situation correctly, and commit the right figurines under pressure.

The larger promise is career momentum. Early work starts with local conflicts and guided contracts, then opens toward league climbing, anomaly clearing, travel, collection growth, and stronger operational reach.

story beat // your role

You solve meaningful conflicts

Your figurines are tools of expertise, not random units. Success comes from reading the conflict and assembling the right answer under constraint.

system beat // combat identity

Draft, commit to exact 12 danger, watch the result

BOG's combat is built around constrained lineup judgment and readable autobattle outcomes, not twitch execution.

the world // story premise

A coherent future, not abstract menu combat

  • Conflicts are framed as sanctioned work inside an official AR duel structure.
  • The world explains why contracts, duels, anomalies, and travel all belong to the same product.
  • The player should feel trusted, capable, and increasingly important inside that system.

the loop // why players stay

One match feeds a broader career arc

  • Take on a conflict, prepare strategically, resolve it, get rewarded, and return stronger.
  • Short sessions still point toward collection growth, better standing, and more ambitious work.
  • The match is only the inner loop; the product promise is a whole career built around it.

first session // what this build teaches

The demo starts with the shortest complete BOG promise

  • You start from a clean player state and enter a guided mission battle.
  • The first session teaches one essential rule: complete the lineup to exactly 12 danger.
  • After the battle, you claim the mission reward, return to the hub, and see that the next step is already waiting.
