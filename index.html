About

Thrylox Game Studio

Thrylox is an independent game development studio focused on high-quality collectible and tactical experiences built with Unreal Engine for mobile and other digital platforms.

Our mission is to combine cinematic visuals, accessible gameplay and transparent monetization into long-lasting live games.

Primary product

Flagship Project

Thrylox is currently developing its first title: a cross-platform, session-based strategy game with collectible heroes and deep team-building mechanics.

  • Built on Unreal Engine with high-fidelity 3D figurines.
  • Designed for iOS and Android, with the option to scale to PC and other platforms.
  • Live-ops ready architecture, analytics-driven balancing and content updates.
Distribution model

Distribution & Monetization

  • Digital-only distribution via official storefronts (Apple App Store, Google Play, PC platforms and partner stores).
  • Free-to-play model with optional in-app purchases (cosmetics, collections, expansions).
  • No gray channels or third-party key resellers.
  • Compliance with platform policies, KYC/AML requirements of payment providers and applicable regulations.
Operations

Studio & Operations

  • Founders: experienced product, analytics and game industry specialists.
  • Remote-first team with contractors and partners across the EU and other regions.
  • Using industry-standard tools: Unreal Engine, GitHub, CI/CD, modern analytics stack.
