about

Thrylox Game Studio

Thrylox is an independent game development studio focused on high-quality collectible and tactical experiences built with Unreal Engine for mobile and other digital platforms.

Our mission is to combine cinematic visuals, accessible gameplay, and transparent monetization into long-lasting live games.

primary product

Flagship Project

Thrylox is developing its first title: a cross-platform, session-based strategy game with collectible heroes and deep team-building mechanics.

  • Built on Unreal Engine with high-fidelity 3D figurines.
  • Designed for iOS and Android, with the option to scale to PC and other platforms.
  • Live-ops ready architecture, analytics-driven balancing, and content updates.

distribution model

Distribution & Monetization

  • Digital-only distribution via official storefronts and partner channels.
  • Free-to-play with optional in-app purchases for cosmetics, collections, and expansions.
  • No gray channels or third-party key resellers.
  • Compliance with platform policies and applicable KYC, AML, and sanctions regulations.

operations

Studio & Operations

  • Founders with product, analytics, and game industry experience.

  • Industry-standard tools including Unreal Engine, GitHub, CI/CD, and analytics infrastructure.
